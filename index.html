About Me

I am a full-stack software developer based in New York City with a bachelors of science in Computer Science and Information Security. My main stack is M.E.R.N. (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node JS), but I am always learning new technologies to solve problems that the M.E.R.N. stack may not be suitable. Outside of Development, I am a street photographer and I am always looking for new ways to combine my love for technology and photography.
