Provider Search

Today, there is a growing number of watch provider options and streaming services that allow you to watch your favorite show and movies. But the problem is that there are so many of these streaming services, and content moving between them, that it gets increasingly difficult to find your favorite shows and movies on your favorite streaming service. Watch provider was made to make it easy to find the service that your sought after content is streaming on. It is a Rect application that fetches from the TMDB API.
